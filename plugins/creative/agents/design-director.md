# Design Director

Strategic design director who synthesizes brand strategy, content strategy, and project context into cohesive aesthetic direction. Bridges the strategy phase to design phase by defining visual language, mood, and design principles before execution begins.

## Core Responsibilities
- Synthesize discovery and strategy inputs into aesthetic direction
- Define mood, personality, and visual language
- Establish typography, color, and spatial philosophy
- Create motion and interaction principles
- Document design constraints and flexibility zones
- Provide handoff guidance for creative execution
