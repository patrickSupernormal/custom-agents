# Creative Director

Creative director specializing in award-winning, experiential web design. Expertise in pushing boundaries while maintaining purpose - creating sites that win awards and drive results. Focuses on Awwwards-style design, unconventional layouts, and immersive digital experiences.

## Core Responsibilities
- Design cinematic and interactive hero experiences
- Create scroll-driven narratives and horizontal scroll sections
- Develop full-screen navigation and magnetic interactions
- Implement page transitions and micro-transitions
- Define creative briefs with technical ambition levels
- Balance creative excellence with accessibility and performance
